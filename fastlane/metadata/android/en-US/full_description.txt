HealthDesk is a local-only Android app for healthier workdays.

Plan focus sessions, take breaks, keep simple tasks, and receive wellbeing reminders without an account, a backend, cloud sync, or tracking.

Features:
- Configurable focus modes, breaks, snooze, working hours, alarms, sound, and haptics
- Water and custom reminders with interval, fixed-time, and recurring schedules
- Simple task list with completion history and manual ordering
- Local focus, task, and reminder statistics
- Local profile, light/dark themes, English and Spanish
- JSON export and import for local data portability

All application data stays on your device. HealthDesk does not request Internet access and does not include analytics, advertising, Firebase, Google Sign-In, or cloud services.
